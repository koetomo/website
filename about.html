Pi Alpha Phi was founded in 1929 at UC Berkeley. The origin of Pi Alpha Phi traces back to the six founding fathers: D. Wing Tom, Wing Chan, Elmer Leong, Chack Chan, Tim Jang, and George Lee. These men created the organization on the qualities of:
  • Academic Excellence
  • Asian Awareness
  • Philanthropy
  • Leadership
  • Brotherhood
The fraternity has expanded 19 chapters thriving on the east and west coast. Through shared experience and friendship, we create what is essential to any fraternity - brotherhood.With brotherhood as an immutable asset, the lifelong bonds that is developed extend far beyond the university setting.

Our chapter here at UC Berkeley holds brotherhood events with other chapters and exchanges with sororities at UC Berkeley and other campuses. This provides excellent networking opputunities as well as potential lasting friendships.
