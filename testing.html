PI ALPHA PHI

In 1929, six young men came together at the University of California, Berkeley to form an organization based on academic excellence, asian awareness, brotherhood, leadership, and philanthropy.

For over 80 years strong, Pi Alpha Phi has been helping like-minded individuals realize these goals and develop into leaders in their communities.
